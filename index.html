📋 Expert Badge Note: AZ-104 Requirement

You can sit and pass the AZ-305 exam without holding AZ-104 — no prerequisite blocks you from registering. However, to receive the full Azure Solutions Architect Expert certification badge, Microsoft also requires an active Azure Administrator Associate (AZ-104) on your transcript. So the practical approach: focus entirely on AZ-305 now, earn the exam pass, then plan AZ-104 as a follow-up milestone to unlock the Expert badge. Solutions Architect Expert requirements →

🎯 AZ-305 Exam Reality Check

One architect who recently re-certified rated AZ-305 difficulty at 6/10 — easier than AZ-104 and AZ-500. The exam is architecture-centric (no coding required), making your .NET/Azure background a genuine advantage. Question types include case studies, multiple-choice, and drag-and-drop architecture diagrams. The key is reasoning from business requirements → Azure service choice, not just knowing service names.
